Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking 3 to 6 months before your wedding date. Because we provide high-touch service and custom configurations (especially for our Premium and Concierge tiers), we strictly limit the number of slots we accept per month to ensure absolute quality.

To reserve your slot, you only need to submit our basic inquiry form with your Name, Wedding Date, Viber Number, and desired Package. We will review availability first. Once confirmed and secured via Viber, we will then guide you on gathering your timeline, photos, and guest list during onboarding.

I personally act as your digital guest list manager, handling all entourage changes, sending you weekly summary reports, and compiling your final headcount for the caterer.

About Me

Hello, my name is Marrsy

It all started when I was planning my own wedding. I realized I wanted a seamless way for our guests to RSVP online instead of dealing with easily lost physical invitations.

A digital RSVP meant it was instantly accessible, and it gave me the freedom to share so much more with our guests, from engagement photos and travel details to our exact celebration colors and dress code.

Seeing how convenient and beautiful a wedding website could be, I decided to create this service. My goal is to help future brides experience that same stress-free elegance, blending stunning design with smart technology so you can focus on enjoying your big day.

I’d love to hear about your vision and how we can create something great together.
